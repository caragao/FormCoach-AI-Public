KineCount

Simple camera-based workout tracking for iPhone.

KineCount helps you count Squats, Push-ups, and Plank hold time using your iPhone camera. The app is designed for short home workout sessions and stores workout information locally on your device.

Privacy Policy Contact Support

What KineCount does

  • Counts Squats and Push-ups using on-device camera-based pose detection.
  • Tracks Plank valid hold time.
  • Shows local workout history, weekly progress, streaks, all-time local totals, and earned minutes.
  • Lets users optionally spend earned workout minutes to temporarily unlock selected apps through App Limits.

Privacy-first design

  • No account required.
  • No backend.
  • No cloud sync.
  • No analytics.
  • No ads.
  • No payments.
  • No social features.
  • No Apple Health integration.
  • No video, image, camera frame, or pose signal upload.
  • Camera processing runs on-device.
  • App Limits does not read app usage data.

App Limits

KineCount may request Screen Time access so the user can select apps and use the optional App Limits feature. The user selects individual apps, earns minutes through workout sessions, and can spend those minutes to temporarily unlock the selected apps.

Category-only blocking is not supported in the current version. KineCount does not know which apps the user actually opens, when, or for how long.

Important limitations

  • KineCount is informational only.
  • Counts, hold time, and estimated calories are camera-based estimates and may be imperfect.
  • Lighting, framing, clothing, body position, and iPhone placement may affect results.
  • KineCount does not provide medical advice, injury prevention, diagnosis, physiotherapy, rehabilitation, form correction, coaching, scoring, clinical analysis, biomechanical analysis, or treatment guidance.
