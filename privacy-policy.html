Privacy Policy for FormCoach AI

Effective date: 2026-05-02

Overview

FormCoach AI is an informational camera-based exercise tracking app.

The current beta supports Squat and Push-up only. Plank is not available in the current beta.

How the app works in the current beta

The app uses the iPhone camera to show a live preview during exercise sessions and estimate repetitions for supported exercises.

Camera processing is intended to happen on the device.

FormCoach AI does not upload workout videos.

Data handling in the current beta

  • The current beta does not require an account.
  • The current beta does not use a backend.
  • The current beta does not include analytics.
  • The current beta does not include payments.
  • The current beta does not include social features.
  • The current beta does not include app blocker functionality.

Feedback and communications

If a tester contacts us by email or submits feedback through TestFlight, we may receive the information they choose to provide, such as comments, screenshots, iPhone model, iOS version, and issue descriptions.

Medical and fitness disclaimer

FormCoach AI does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, injury prevention, physiotherapy guidance, correct-form assessment, or biomechanical assessment.
